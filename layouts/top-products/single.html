Use Cases




Customized Data Analytics Dashboard

Your company wants a customized web interface to monitor business operations and to conduct some simple exploratory data analysis. You know all the questions you want to ask your data, but you do not have the in house personel to build web dashboards. Tychobra can build the dashboard to your specifications and handle hosting and server configuration. As your business needs evolve, we will work with your team to tweak and improve the dashboard. You will have lifetime access to the code used to build the dashboard, and your company can choose your level of invlovment in the actual coding process


Machine Learning

Your company wants in on the recent advances in AI and machine learning and you've heard R is the state of the art data science platform. Your company has analysts who work in R, but they do not have the web development experience to communicate their R models as interactive web applications. Tychobra will take your models and build a Shiny app that allows your team to make decisions based on model output. As always, your team has lifetome access to the code used to build the Shiny app.


Plug into GCP and AWS

You have some Shiny apps, and you are looking to super charge them with the offerings on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Amazon Web Services (AWS). You would like to hit the ground running, but no one at your company has experience working with these cloud providers. We will take care of configuring, monitoring, and maintaining your app on either of the platforms. You access your apps through the simple Tychobra Cloud Platform interface.


Share Shiny app with multiple authentication levels

Your company has a few internal Shiny apps that you want to share accross the company and with select 3rd parties. Control who is granted access to your app, and set access permissions so the invited user can only use the app ("Viewer" authorization level), the user can use the app and develop application features ("Developer" authorization level), or allow the user to use, develop, and administer the app ("Admin" authorization level).
