Customized Data Analytics Dashboard

Your company wants a customized web interface to monitor business operations and to conduct some simple exploratory data analysis. You know all the questions you want to ask your data, but you do not have the in house personel to build web dashboards. Tychobra can build the dashboard to your specifications and handle hosting and server configuration. As your business needs evolve, we will work with your team to tweak and improve the dashboard. You will own the code used to build the dashboard, and your company can choose your level of invlovment in the actual coding process


Machine Learning

Your company wants in on the recent advances in AI and machine learning and you've heard R is the state of the art data science platform. You have some analysts who work in R, but they do not have the web development experience to communicate their models as interactive web applications. Tychobra will take the currently under utilized models and build an interactive Shiny app that allows management to derive value from the data. As always, your team will own all the code used to build the Shiny app, so your analysts can take on a larger role in the Shiny development as they learn more about it.
